About Halloluwa Tours

Welcome to Halloluwa Tours, your premier travel partner specializing in curated group tours from Sri Lanka to Asia's most captivating destinations. We believe that travel is best shared, creating memories that last a lifetime.


Our passion is crafting seamless, enriching, and fun-filled group travel experiences. From handling all the logistics to providing expert local guides, we ensure your journey is comfortable, safe, and truly authentic. Join our community of happy travelers and let us show you the magic of Thailand, the diversity of India, the modernity of Singapore, and the beauty of Malaysia.

Our Destinations

Thailand

The Land of Smiles awaits! Explore bustling markets, serene temples, and stunning beaches. A perfect blend of culture, cuisine, and adventure.

India

A vibrant tapestry of cultures, colors, and history. From the iconic Taj Mahal to the spiritual banks of the Ganges, India's diversity will mesmerize you.

Singapore

Discover the futuristic "Lion City." Marvel at stunning architecture, indulge in world-class shopping, and savor a melting pot of culinary delights.

Malaysia

A country of contrasts, where lush rainforests meet bustling cities. Explore cultural heritage in Penang and the modern marvel of Kuala Lumpur.
